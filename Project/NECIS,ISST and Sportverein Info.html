NECIS/ISST and Sportverein Info

NECIS:

NECIS stands for Northwest European Council of International Schools. It began as a small thing across three schools for just basketball, volleyball, and football. It has grown into a system with 11 schools, 4 age groups and 9 sports including:

  • Basketball
  • Football
  • Volleyball
  • Swimming
  • Tennis
  • Golf
  • Softball
  • Track and Field
  • Touch Rugby
